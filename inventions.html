🛠️ Inventions & Weapons

These are creations born in the mind of Daksh Singh — tools, technologies, and weapons never seen before. Imaginative. Impossible. And yet, real in the Daverse.

💣 Pressure Bubbles
A powerful weapon where air is compressed inside a water shell. If an enemy cuts it — boom — the compressed air bursts with force. It’s a trap and a bomb at once.
🍳 The Uncooker
A reverse microwave! It can make hot food cold instantly without freezing. Useful for saving energy, preserving taste, and doing the impossible.
🦷 Teeth Scanner
A super-accurate scanner using teeth instead of eyes or fingerprints — even twins have different teeth! A whole new level of security.
🔥 Heated Eye Laser
A scanner that turns into a weapon. If the person scanned is unauthorized, the harmless beam turns into a focused laser that can trigger alarms or heat up defenses.
🪵 Solid Stitcher
A revolutionary tool that can rejoin non-metal solids like wood, plastic, or stone — not with glue, but by rebuilding the material's internal structure on a molecular level. It restores the object as if it was never cut in the first place.
